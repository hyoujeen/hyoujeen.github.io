Hello !

Hi, I'm You Jeen (유진)!

My interests lie within and between the fields of computer science, philosophy, and technology governance. And as a Korean American, I seek to help foster cross-cultural collaboration at the intersection of these fields.

When I'm not working, I decompress by digital painting, taking scenic photos, meditating, and running with my dog.
